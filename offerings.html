Your Ultimate Retreat

Welcome to Villa Patron, a stunning 200m² modern villa featuring a spacious 60m² balcony that spans the length of the house. The villa is surrounded by a beautiful 400m² garden. Inside, sleek design meets luxurious comfort, offering the perfect retreat for relaxation and indulgence.

A Gateway to Comfort

Villa Patron features a modern kitchen and four spacious en-suite rooms, offering comfort for up to 8 guests. Located at the foot of Kozjak Mountain, it’s only minutes away from the beach, Split, and the airport, making it a convenient yet tranquil retreat.

Where Fun Meets Serenity

Discover fun and relaxation with a private playground, a gym, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Whether you’re looking for adventure or quiet moments with loved ones, Villa Patron promises an unforgettable escape for families and friends.
